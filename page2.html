The Sunshine Girl

Hodaka sees Hina being intimidated into working at a back-alley club.
After a brief chase, he scares off the club owners by firing his gun into the air,
thinking it was a toy.

He and Hina escape; she takes him to Yoyogi Kaikan,
an abandoned building with a shrine on the roof, where he throws the gun away.

Hina astonishes Hodaka by demonstrating her ability to clear the sky by praying.
